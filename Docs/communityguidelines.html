--- layout: default title: "Community Guidelines" permalink: /community-guidelines/ --- # Loppis Community Guidelines Welcome to the Loppis community! We're excited to have you here and are committed to fostering a respectful and engaging environment for everyone. Please review these guidelines to ensure that all interactions remain positive and constructive. ## 1. Respect and Courtesy - **Be respectful:** Treat fellow users with kindness and courtesy at all times. - **Constructive dialogue:** Engage in thoughtful discussions and offer feedback that is constructive and helpful. - **Inclusive language:** Use inclusive language that welcomes and respects all members of our community. ## 2. Content and Contributions - **Share original work:** Post content that you have created or that you have permission to share. - **Appropriate content:** Ensure that all shared content is appropriate, free from offensive or discriminatory language, and respectful of others. - **No spam:** Avoid posting repetitive content, unsolicited promotions, or advertisements. ## 3. Privacy and Safety - **Personal information:** Do not share personal or sensitive information about yourself or others. - **Respect privacy:** Be mindful of the privacy of fellow community members. Report any attempts to compromise privacy immediately. ## 4. Engagement and Collaboration - **Constructive discussions:** Focus on adding value through thoughtful insights, questions, and supportive responses. - **Report concerns:** If you encounter content or behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it to our moderators or use the designated feedback channels. - **Help and support:** Assist new members in navigating the community and contribute to a welcoming environment. ## 5. Consequences of Violations Failure to adhere to these guidelines may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent exclusion from the community, depending on the severity and frequency of the violation. ## 6. Updates to Guidelines We may update these community guidelines from time to time. Your continued participation in the Loppis community constitutes your acceptance of any changes made.
