Delete Your Data

We understand that privacy is important. If you decide that you no longer want your personal data to be stored on Loppis, please follow the steps below to request deletion of your information.

How to Delete Your Data

1. Delete Your Account Through Settings

  • Log in to Your Account: Access your Loppis account using your login credentials.
  • Navigate to Account Settings: Go to your account settings where you can manage your personal information.
  • Locate the Delete Account Option: Find and select the "Delete Account" option. This may be under a "Privacy" or "Security" section.
  • Confirm Your Request: Follow the prompts to confirm your decision. Remember, this action is irreversible.

2. Contact Support Directly

If you are unable to delete your account via the settings page, or if you have any special requests regarding your data, please contact our support team:

  • Email: Send a message to support@loppisapp.com with the subject "Delete My Data."
  • Include Account Details: Provide necessary details to help us identify your account and process your request.
  • Confirmation: Our support team will confirm once your data has been removed from our active systems.

3. Processing and Data Retention

  • Processing Time: Please allow up to 30 days for your deletion request to be fully processed.
  • Legal and Regulatory Retention: In some cases, we may be required to retain certain data (such as transaction records) for legal or regulatory purposes even after account deletion. This information will not be accessible or used for any other purpose.

Important Considerations

  • Irreversible Action: Deleting your account and associated data is permanent. Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered.
  • Backup Systems: While your personal information will be removed from our active systems, some data may remain in backups for a limited period as required by our retention policies.
  • Privacy Policy: For additional details on how we handle and protect your data, please review our Privacy Policy.

We are committed to ensuring your privacy and making the data deletion process as clear as possible. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please do not hesitate to contact our support team.
